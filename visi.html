Vision & Mission


Club Name: Infernal

About Club: Welcome to the Infernal Brotherhood, a community of riders bound by a shared passion for motorcycles and the open road. Founded on the principles of camaraderie and adventure, our club offers a space for riders of all backgrounds to come together and experience the thrill of the ride. At the Infernal Brotherhood, we believe in the power of motorcycles to forge connections and create lasting memories. Whether we're embarking on epic road trips, exploring scenic routes, or simply enjoying the camaraderie of fellow riders, every journey with our club is an opportunity for adventure and friendship. Join us and become a part of our vibrant community, where the spirit of the road and the bond of brotherhood unite to create unforgettable experiences. Ride with us, and together, let's embrace the freedom of the open road and the joy of motorcycling.

Vision: To be the premier destination for motorcycle enthusiasts, fostering a vibrant community united by a passion for motorcycles, camaraderie, and adventure.

Mission:

  • Community Building: Foster a welcoming and inclusive environment where riders of all backgrounds can come together to connect, share experiences, and forge meaningful friendships.
  • Promotion of Motorcycling: Promote the joy and excitement of motorcycling by organizing rides, events, and activities that showcase the thrill of the ride and the freedom of the open road.
  • Safety Advocacy: Prioritize safety education and awareness among our members, providing resources, training, and support to help riders ride responsibly and confidently.
  • Camaraderie and Support: Cultivate a strong sense of camaraderie and support within our community, where members can rely on each other for assistance, encouragement, and companionship both on and off the road.
  • Adventure and Exploration: Encourage members to embrace the spirit of adventure by organizing a variety of rides and trips that explore new destinations, scenic routes, and challenging terrains.
  • Lifelong Passion for Riding: Inspire a lifelong passion for motorcycling by providing memorable experiences, fostering a love for the motorcycle lifestyle, and nurturing a sense of belonging within our club.

Aktivitas Klub:

  • Group Rides: Organize regular group rides that cater to riders of all skill levels and interests. From leisurely cruises along scenic routes to challenging adventures through rugged terrains, our group rides offer something for everyone. These rides provide opportunities for members to connect, share their love for motorcycles, and explore new destinations together while fostering a sense of camaraderie and belonging within the club.
  • Workshops and Skill-Building Sessions: Host workshops and skill-building sessions focused on enhancing riders' knowledge and proficiency in various aspects of motorcycling. Topics may include motorcycle maintenance and repair, riding techniques, safety practices, and more. These hands-on learning opportunities not only empower members to become better riders but also promote a culture of continuous learning and improvement within the club.
  • Community Events and Charity Rides: Organize community events and charity rides that give back to the local community and support causes we're passionate about. Whether it's participating in charity fundraisers, organizing food drives, or volunteering for community clean-up initiatives, these events allow members to make a positive impact while representing the values of the Infernal Brotherhood. By coming together to support meaningful causes, we strengthen the bonds of brotherhood and demonstrate the positive influence of the motorcycling community.

Member / Brother: Prospective members of the Infernal Brotherhood should demonstrate a genuine passion for motorcycling, respect for fellow riders, a commitment to safety, active participation in club activities, a willingness to contribute positively to the club, and alignment with its core values of camaraderie, adventure, respect, and safety.
